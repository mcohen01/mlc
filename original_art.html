Title: The Wailing Wall
Artist: Mark L. Cohen
Size: 6' x 9'
Description: Acrylic on Canvas
Created: 2018
Price: $9,000

Mark L. Cohen's paintings, drawings, and monoprints use contemporary and past iconic figures and historical events from popular culture. Subject matter includes war and peace, antisemitism, racism, police brutality, misogyny, LGBTQ+ issues, and more. Proceeds from sales benefit the Cohen Family Foundation for the Arts (CFF) for its mission of education to fight the above social issues. CFF is a not-for-profit 501 (c) (3) organization, making donations tax deductible.
